Domestic Cat

This ferocious beast easily burrows its way into the human heart and sinks its claws in. Most humans have no defense.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12 - 18 years
  • Habitat: Wherever they want.

Cats come in a basic four paw, one tail and one head format with a number of variations on each. Color can range from white to orange to black with stripes, patches and solids added to the mix. Paws and tail can be short or long and thick or thin. The head can range from round to oval to triangular. Finally, a cat's fur can range from none to curly to very long.
